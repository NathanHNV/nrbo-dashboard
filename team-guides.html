High Net Value Desk

Specialized service layer above standard customer support, dedicated to high-value players. Staffed by VIP Account Managers and Retention agents.

Version: SOP 1.2 Owner: Arik Weiss Effective: 03 Nov 2025 Next review: 01 Jun 2026

Our mission: Deliver proactive, personalized service with predictable processes and transparent payments so players feel known. HNV success means players trust us and feel VIP treatment consistently.

Core operating principles

One-team collaboration

VIP and Retention teams work as one unit with clear ownership boundaries. Handovers are explicit and documented so the next handler can pick up within seconds.

Every high-value player is a shared responsibility of the HNV Desk, even though a single owner is accountable at any given time. For Platinum and Black, each player is assigned to a specific VIP Account Manager (their portfolio). If something urgent happens when the owner is unavailable, another team member steps in and informs the owner via a tagged handover note.

Compliance & responsible gaming first

No contact during RG or Compliance holds. Follow Communication Standards Annex B at all times.

Personalisation

Agents can use templates, but always customize them to match the player and purpose. Every outbound message or call ends with a clear invitation or next step — for example, suggesting a time for the next session, offering to call at a convenient time, or presenting a bonus that requires the player's action. Avoid generic "come back soon" pleas; give the player a concrete path to follow.

Proactive engagement with next steps

HNV service is proactive rather than reactive. We reach out to players before they ask, and we always conclude each player interaction with a defined next step or follow-up scheduled.

Each proactive touch should aim to:

  • Shorten time to the next session or conversation
  • Pre-empt friction on deposits, withdrawals, or KYC
  • Strengthen the relationship with well-timed recognition
  • Capture discovery and log it in Player Discovery Information (Annex E)
  • End with a clear next step and time when appropriate

We measure results with connection rate, follow-through on next steps, reactivations won, upgrades achieved, and qualitative feedback.

Upgrade process

→ Gold (Silver to Gold)

Retention Agent upgrades directly once criteria are met. Retention Lead approval required for edge cases.

→ Platinum (Gold to Platinum)

Retention Agent recommends via Retention Lead → VIP Lead reviews and approves → Handover to VIP Account Manager.

On upgrade to Platinum, the Retention Agent hands over all relevant context (discovery, preferences, recent activity, open issues) so the VIP AM can seamlessly continue the relationship. The new VIP AM must make a welcome contact within the same shift and set a next step.

→ Black (Platinum to Black)

VIP Account Manager prepares a case → vetted by VIP Lead with senior management.

Black upgrade approvals are delivered personally by the VIP Account Manager. All upgrades must be documented in BB notes with date, owner, and reasoning for edge cases.

VIP account managers

Own Platinum and Black portfolios. Focus on long-term relationships, proactive outreach, and tier transitions.

Core responsibilities

  • Relationship management — maintain long-term personal relationships. Know preferences and contact windows. Provide high-touch, rapid service.
  • Proactive outbound (voice-first) — reserve daily outbound blocks. Aim for quality conversations leading to next steps. Call first for saves, detractors, and significant reactivations.
  • Account monitoring — monitor portfolio activity and milestones. Prepare Black upgrade cases and deliver approvals personally.
  • Issue advocacy — when cases exceed authority, escalate to VIP Lead. Remain the player's single point of contact.
  • Cross-brand portfolio — one human owner per human across all sister brands when feasible.

Retention agents

Own High-Value Silver and Gold. Focus on structured outbound cadences, inbound Gold support, and real-time monitoring.

Core responsibilities

  • Structured proactive cadences — run upgrade nurturing and reactivation cadences with personalized touches. Prioritize the 8–21 day inactivity window.
  • Inbound Gold support — handle Gold inbound faster than general support. Convert service moments into relationship moments.
  • Save and reactivation — execute saves and win-back attempts with clear next steps.
  • Real-time monitoring — watch dashboards for failed deposits, spikes, big wins, or RG signals. Pause outreach if RG/Compliance flags appear.

Inbound service & overflow

Tier-based routing

  • Black and Platinum → VIP Account Managers (first line)
  • Gold → Retention Agents (first line)

Email routing (Dixa)

Use the player's cross-brand max VIP level for routing. Do not auto-assign inbound emails to the named VIP Account Manager. Urgent matters (compliance, tech blockers, withdrawal delays, detractors) are handled immediately by whoever is on shift.

Ad-hoc urgent handover

Urgent issues are a shared HNV responsibility. If the primary team has no capacity, the other team picks up immediately and keeps the player updated until handover back to the portfolio owner is confirmed.

What counts as urgent:

  • Compliance or RG states that block contact or play (KYC lock, AML review)
  • Tech issues that block login or play
  • Withdrawal delays or failures raised by the player
  • Detractors and major complaints requiring voice first

How to act: Claim the case in Dixa → log CBO contact note → add Billboard note "Urgent: [type]" with named owner and expiry → post in #hnv-handover → tag portfolio owner. Clear the BB note after resolution.

Offer hierarchy & caps

Hierarchy: Recognition (no conditions) → Free spins on favourites → Cash gift (last resort, requires next step and lead approval).

Recognition vs goodwill

Recognition — given solely to celebrate loyalty, a milestone, or positive event (birthday, upgrade, big win). Can be no-strings if tagged "Recognition Gift" and within policy. No next step required.

Goodwill — any value transfer for retention or recovery purposes. Every goodwill item must be part of a plan with a logged next step and follow-up time. Lead approval needed for large or exceptional cases.

Cash is a last resort. Every goodwill grant (not recognition) must have a documented next step. QA checks for ≥95% compliance.

Escalation & priority matrix

P1

Urgent blocker

Payment block, outage on key provider, WD SLA breach, VIP detractor pending contact. First contact: immediate by Call → WhatsApp. Update cadence: every 60 min until stable, then by promise time.

P2

High-priority friction

Repeated failed deposits, WD delay beyond normal, KYC stall on VIP. First contact: ≤30 min during on-duty hours. Update cadence: every 2 hrs until ETA given, then by promise time.

P3

Routine follow-ups

Non-urgent asks and standard follow-ups. First contact: inside SLA per VIP tier. Update cadence: by next promised time in note. Normal workflow.

How to log an escalation

Open the HNV Escalation sheet immediately when you escalate to Payments, Compliance, Tech, or a Lead. Required fields:

  • Opened at — exact time of first escalation
  • Priority — P1, P2, or P3
  • Category and issue summary — one factual line, no predictions
  • Owner — transfers only on a tagged, acknowledged handover
  • CBO link and Slack/Zendesk thread
  • Next player update promised at — concrete time told to the player
  • Lead approval and resolved at — record when lead signs off and issue closes

Handover & ownership

Handover template

[PlayerID/link] – [Issue] – [Promise/deadline] – [Tagged new owner] – [Next step: exact time window]

Ownership rules

  • Ownership starts when you engage the issue or player
  • Ownership transfers only when you tag a named on-duty owner in #hnv-handover and they acknowledge (👍 in Slack)
  • If the tagged new owner becomes unavailable, another team member takes over and updates accordingly

06:01 @B (incoming): 👍

07:56 @B: "Payments confirmed withdrawal will be paid in 1h. Informed player."

08:22 @B: "Payment confirmed. Updated player via WhatsApp."

Channel channels to know

  • #vip-alerts — big wins, urgent issues
  • #withdrawal-questions — coordination with Payments

Never share personal player data in Slack. Use player IDs or account links only.

KPIs & quality

Team KPIs

  • Deposit / NRBO uplift — VIP team: Platinum/Black. Retention: Gold.
  • Tier mobility — successful upgrades Silver→Gold and Gold→Platinum per quarter

Individual KPIs — soft (coaching)

  • Follow-up rate — complete required cadence steps daily
  • Conversion / save rate — ~12% benchmark of targeted players depositing within 7 days
  • Individual QA / CSAT trend
  • Workload balance
  • Discovery capture rate
  • Time-to-welcome (Platinum upgrade)
  • Handover quality
  • SLA adherence

Individual KPIs — hard (gate to team bonus)

Failing a hard gate disqualifies the agent from that period's team bonus share even if the team pool pays.

  • Compliance: zero red-line breaches per CS SOP
  • QA minimum: quarterly pass rate ≥ 80%
  • Attendance: meets Attendance Policy
  • Documentation: CBO note hygiene meets Annex E in spot checks
  • Next-step compliance: ≥ 95% on goodwill grants
  • Voice connect volume: VIP AMs avg 6 connects/shift; Retention avg 2 connects/shift

Glossary

Acknowledgement (Ack)
A simple confirmation (e.g. 👍 in Slack) that a tagged owner has taken responsibility after a handover. Ownership transfers only after ack or at shift change.
BB note
Billboard note — temporary, high-visibility banner on a player profile. Must include owner and expiry. Cleared when resolved.
CBO
Central system of record. Every significant contact is logged here. If it's not in CBO, it didn't happen.
Channel Matrix
Table setting Primary → Backup → Fallback channels by segment and scenario. Player's recorded CBO preference overrides the matrix.
Detractor
A player who gave negative feedback or low CSAT. Treated as high-impact; recovery prioritized, usually voice first for VIP.
End-to-end ownership
The engager owns the issue until a confirmed handover or shift change.
Goodwill
Any value transfer (FS, chip, cash as last resort) for recognition or recovery. Every goodwill item must have a logged next step. Lead approval for large cases.
HNV
High Net Value. The HNV Desk sits above standard support to serve high-value players with proactive, personalized service.
Light loop
Shortened reactivation sequence used when a player briefly returns and goes silent again. Shifts focus from "wake up" to "sustain momentum."
Next step
The specific follow-up action and time window that ends every interaction. Logged in CBO. QA requires ≥95% of goodwill to have a documented next step.
NRBO
Net Revenue Before Overhead. Used for HNV performance views in CBO dashboards.
Portfolio
The set of assigned Platinum or Black players owned by a VIP Account Manager for proactive care and decisions.
Recognition
A gesture given solely to celebrate loyalty, a milestone, or positive event. No conditions or pressure. Tag "Recognition Gift" in CBO. No next step required.
Reactivation
Outreach to regain an inactive player. The 8–21 day window is the sweet spot for conversion impact.
Save
Post-withdrawal re-engagement. Celebratory tone after wins, caring tone after tough runs. Always log the attempt and next step.
